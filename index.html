About Us

Bretmor Headwear is a U.S. based sales office for some of the best headwear manufacturers in the World.

Since 2002 we have specialized in creating a streamlined process for importing directly from these manufacturers. Our team has over 150 years of combined experience in the sportswear apparel industry, and over time we have developed a workflow that utilizes that expertise to make designing and ordering custom hats a smooth and easy experience.

With full time artists on staff, we will assist in designing and manufacturing custom headwear for your specific needs.

We are committed to providing friendly, dependable, and worry-free service while supplying our customers with the finest quality headwear at very competitive prices.

Contact us at 1-800-800-4225 or info@bretmor.com to learn more and to begin your new headwear design!

Our Process

We make the design process of creating your custom headwear painless. Our in-house Account Managers are dedicated to working directly with you, and ensuring your new hat is customized exactly to your needs.

This Process can be broken down into 3 Stages:

1. Choose Your Style
View Styles

There are a lot of factors that go into designing a hat, and the crown style is where it all begins! This will determine the overall shape, structure, and look of the Cap and Visor


2. Select Your Fabric
View Fabrics

The Fabric determines the Look and Feel of the Cap through different Colors and Materials. During this step, an account manager will work with you to determine the best options based on your needs.


3. Choose Your Decoration
View Decorations

Our wide selection of Decorations includes various Embroidery and Patch options, which will allow you to place your own Logo, Text, or Artwork on the Hat in any location of your choice.

You will now be ready to finalize your order!

Cap Styles

We offer a variety of different hat structures and shapes to fit your specific needs. Below are some of our most popular options, with additional specialty styles available upon request when you contact us!

5-Panel Caps

5-Panel Caps have a partial seam in the center of the Cap Front. This allows for freedom to decorate the front panel without regard to a seam running through the logo. This is especially useful for Screen Print and Sublimation Print designs. 5-Panel Caps are made in a variety of crown heights and with your choice of fabrics/trims to customize your design.

View All Types
*6-Panel Caps*

6-Panel Caps are our MOST POPULAR Style. They are made with a full seam in the center of the Cap Front. This style is very versatile for the ball game, beach, uniforms, etc. 6-Panel Caps are made in a variety of crown heights and our Account Managers can help you to select the best shape for your unique needs.

View All Types
7-Panel Caps

7-Panel Caps feature seams that split the front of the hat into three separate panels, with a large seamless front panel great for complex logos. Many customers also choose to use multiple colors/fabrics to express their activewear design style.

View All Types
Camo Caps

While not a specific style of cap, Camouflage designs do deserve their own recognition. We offer a vast selection of licensed camo patterns which may be used in most of our headwear styles. Check out our complete selection of Mossy Oak, Realtree, and Kryptek patterns as well as some non-licensed options.

View All Types
Full Camo Library
Camper Caps

The sporty Camper Cap offers a seamless front panel to display your logo. Mix and match your favorite fabrics and colors to complement your outdoor adventure!

View All Types
Visors

Visor styles are available in multiple crown heights. Contact our team to learn more about the available options to best fit your logo.

View All Types
Bucket and Boonie Hats

These wide brimmed hats are perfect for providing shade on a sunny summer day! They are fully customizable down to the material and color of the draw-string, and can feature solid fabric as well as mesh options.

View All Types
Fleece Beanies

Fleece Beanies are a great option for keeping your head and ears warm in the colder winter and fall months! This style is available in solid colors as well as licensed camouflage patterns.

View All Types
Knit Beanies

Our cozy Knit Beanies can feature solid color and reversible designs. You may also have your own logo/artwork woven into the material of the beanie itself. This style of beanie is offered in multiple lengths, with or without a cuff.
