About Us

Bretmor Headwear is a U.S. based Direct Sales Office for the world's largest Hat Manufacturers.

With over 75 years of cap manufacturing experience, these companies produce over 50% of the world's headwear.

We specialize in creating a flawless process in importing directly from these companies. Our staff has over 50 years of combined experience in the sportswear apparel industry and thus can make this process smooth and efficient. With full time artists on staff, we are able to assist in designing and manufacturing a customized embroidered hat for your specific needs.

We are committed to providing friendly, dependable and worry free service while supplying our customers with the finest quality headwear at very competitive prices.

Our Process

We make the design process of creating your custom headwear painless. Our in-house Designers are dedicated to working directly with you, and ensuring your new hat is customized exactly to your needs.

This Process can be broken down into 5 Stages:

1. Choose Your Style
View Styles

There are a lot of factors that go into designing a hat, and the crown style is where it all begins! This will determine the overall shape, structure, and look of the Cap and Visor


2. Select Your Fabric
View Fabrics

The Fabric determines the Look and Feel of the Cap through different Colors and Materials. During this step, an account manager will work with you to determine the best options based on your needs.


3. Choose Your Decoration
View Decorations

Our wide selection of Decorations includes various Embroidery and Patch options, which will allow you to place your own Logo, Text, or Artwork on the Hat in any location of your choice.

You will now be ready to finalize your order!

Cap Styles

We offer a variety of different hat structures and shapes to fit your specific needs. Below are some of our most popular options, with additional specialty styles available upon request when you contact us!

5-Panel Caps

5-Panel Caps have a partial seam in the center of the cap front. Caps may be made in solid fabric, or with a mesh back.

View All Types
*6-Panel Caps*

6-Panel Caps have a full seam in the center of the cap front. Caps may be made in solid fabric, or with a mesh back.

View All Types
7-Panel Caps

7-Panel Caps, also known as the "Admiral" style, feature seams that split the front of the hat into three seperate panels, with a large area dedicated to the front panel. The front of the hat is relatively rigid, flat, and large, making for a great area to display complex logos and other artwork.

View All Types
Camo Hats

While not a specific style of hat, we offer a vast selection of licensed camo patterns which can be applied to most of our available hat structures. Check out our complete selection of fabrics for a complete display of the camo patterns we offer.

View All Types
Camper Hats

Similar to the 7-Panel Style, the camper hat offers a split seam that divides the front of the cap into three sections. The overall shape of the hat is less rigid and more sporty overall than the standard 7-Panel Style.

View All Types
Visors

We are happy to offer totally customizable visors, which can be made entirely out of solid fabrics or with a mesh back.

View All Types
Bucket and Boonie Hats

These wide brimmed hats are perfect for providing shade on a sunny day! They are fully customizeable down to the material and color of the draw-string, can can feature solid fabric from any of our available libraries as well as mesh.

View All Types
Fleece Beanies

Fleece Beanies are a great option for keeping your ears warm in the colder months! This type of beanie can be made both with and without a cuff, depending on your needs.

View All Types
Knit Beanies

Our Knit Beanies are made with great care, and can feature custom or licensed patterns and artwork woven into the material of the beanie itself. This style of beanie can come with or without a cuff, and also has an available reversable option.
