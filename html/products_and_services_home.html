Straight Stairlifts

By far the most simplistic and common type of stairlift, straight stairlifts consist of a straight rail, chassis, footplate, and seat. These are typically used in private residences with straight, standard staircases. The rail is measured and cut to length for each staircase, and is bolted to the steps securely. Straight stairlifts are generally the most affordable type of stairlift, and installation can be scheduled within days of placing the order for the rail, chassis and seat.

Curved Stairlifts

Curved-rail Stairlifts are custom made, from careful measurements taken from a survey of your staircase, to conform completely to the shape of an individual staircase. These are for staircases that turn corners or are otherwise not completely straight. On staircases with landings they eliminate the need for multiple straight stairlifts, instead providing one continuous ride up the entire length of the staircase.

Vehicle Scooter Lifts

The Vehicle Scooter Lifts we provide are designed for personal vehicles, for personal use. They are motorized platforms built to lift and carry most models of electric scooter, either within or outside of your vehicle depending on the model you choose. The external models are secured to the trailer hitch and sit off of the back of your vehicle, however internal options are also available for vans

Grab Bars

Designed to enable a person to maintain balance, hold some of their weight while maneuvering, or have something to grab onto in case of a slip or fall, Grab Bars are a simple way to provide accessibility and safety in treacherous areas such as a bathroom or staircase.

Ramps

We offer both permanent and temprary varieties of wheelchair and scooter ramps. Our modular ramps come in sectional pieces of sturdy alluminum, and can be arranged to fit whatever height and layout regulation requires for your staircase, where the foldable portable ramps are more temporary and portable.

Ceiling Lifts

These lifts are secured to the ceiling, and feature a hanging sling that moves along the track in which the user sits. They are designed to be easily connected, disconnected, and transferred between a wide variety of track systems. The sling itself features a motor unit to easily suspend the sling with the push of a button, and a carry bar is included with each unit we provide.
