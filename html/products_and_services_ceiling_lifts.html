Portable Ceiling Lifts
The Prism portable ceiling lifts that we provide are built to be easily used by a single caregiver. Measurements based off of your needs are taken, and a track cut to the specific length needed is secured to the ceiling. The track is then anchored into the beams in the ceiling, which we reinforce as necessary. The motorized lift and sling is then hung from the track on rollers, allowing the cargiver to move and transition the user to either their destination or seperate tracks with minimal effort.
  • Comes in several weight capacities: 300 lbs, 440 lbs, and 600 lbs
  • Simple maintence
  • Small and compact
  • Lightest portable lift available
  • Compatible with existing track systems
  • Most affordable ceiling lift available
