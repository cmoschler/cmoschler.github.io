Simplicity 950
Considered our standard straight stairlift, the simplicity 950 provides a safe and cost effective solution to anyone with a single, straight flight of stairs not requiring powered options.
  • The upholstery is available in sand colored, wipe clean, flame retardant fabric
  • foldable armrests and seat
  • Manual foldable footrest
  • Manual seat swivel and lock
  • Child safety lock
  • Seat safety belt
  • Optional Hinged or Power Slide track

Simplicity Plus
The Simplicity+ model comes with a smart seat, boasting optional powered features such as power swivel and a powered footplate.
  • The upholstery is available in a selection of six different colors
  • Provides the choice of manual or powered folding footplate and swivel options
  • Child safety lock
  • Seat safety belt
  • Optional Hinged or Power Slide track

1000 Outdoor
Similar to a standard 950, our outdoor models are built for a single, straight stairway. Much like the standard straight lifts, they consist of a straight rail, chassis, footplate, and seat. However the track, as well as the upholstery on the seat, are both altered in design to be extremely weatherproof. The rail is measured and cut to match the length of your stairway, and then secured to the steps. Brick or concrete steps are no problem.
  • Fully waterproof and weatherproof Slate Grey apholstery
  • Comes with a waterproof cover
  • Child safety lock
  • Seat safety belt
