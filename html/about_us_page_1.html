ABOUT DP STAIRLIFT

You are in good hands. DP Stairlift is run and managed by one of the most experienced professionals you will find in the field of installing and servicing mobility equipment. As a family owned and operated business, we know exactly how important it is to keep yourself and your loved ones living safely. This is what we strive to achieve for each and every one of our clients through providing and maintaining easy to use, safe products. Each brand of equipment we choose to work with is picked based on the quality and reliability of the product by the company's founder, Dale Yeatts, who has more than thirty years of experience working with them.

We are a mobile service, and come to you when an appointment is made for evaluation, maintenence, or installation of a product. All you have to do is give us a call, and we'll take care of the rest!
