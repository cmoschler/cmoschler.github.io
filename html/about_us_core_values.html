MISSION AND CORE VALUES

Our Mission and Core Values are statements that define the way in which we conduct business, as well as who we are as a company. They help us stay focused, and achieve our goals. They set the foundation for everything we do, and how we handle ourselves professionally.

Mission

To allow our clients to become independent within and outside of their homes again, by providing the product that best meets their needs.

Core Values

Commitment - Careful consideration is taken when deciding what course of action would best suit a client's needs. Once that decision is mutually agreed upon, we support it wholeheartedly.

Integrity - We strive to be reliable, and always keep our promises.

Flexibility - In this field, we are used to the fact that there are many circumstances that may arise to conflict with a scheduled appointment. We aim to remain as flexible as possible and work around your schedule and any unexpected changes in plan.

Respect - When running a business that provides a service which requires access to the clients' personal home, we are absolutely required to respect that space and privacy. We are extremely conscious of the environment of our clients' households, and pride ourselves in leaving them unscathed. Once we leave, there should be no evidence of us being there at all. Aside from the remaining mobility product, of course.
