Modular
Infinitely more affordable than wood or concrete options, EZ Access' modular ramps provide a long term solution to homes and businesses lacking wheelchair accessible entrances. Each ramp is made of highly durable aluminum, and constructed with sectional pieces based off of measurments taken from your staircase. This allows for extreme flexibility in the layout of the ramp, and quick installation.
  • Extremely flexible and able to conform to nearly any layout
  • Ramps and platforms made of lightweight, sturdy alluminum
  • No anchoring into concrete footings or pads is required, ramps are free standing
  • Easily removed, modified, or re-configured once installed
  • Extruded, skid resistant surface
  • Low maintenence once installed, never rusts like steel or rots like wooden alternatives
  • 850lb weight limit

Portable
These tri-fold ramps from EZ-Access vary in sizes ranging from 5' to 10' in length, and provide an excellent solution to raised landings, steps, or larger vehicles.
  • Unique 3-fold design makes it extremely portable
  • Slip resistant surface
  • Self-adjusting bottom transition plates
  • Can be seperated into two individual sections
