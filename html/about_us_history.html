OUR HISTORY

The owner of DP Stairlift Company, Dale Yeatts, has been installing stairlifts for 32 years in Richmond, VA, and the surrounding areas. Dale worked as a technician with many of the top mobility centers the city has to offer throughout his life, before eventually deciding to strike out on his own and found his own company in the industry out of a desire to better help the people he had grown to love. With the reigns in his hands, he could oversee every aspect of his field, and take a more direct, hands-on approach that has since led to thousands of satisfied customers.

However, he is not alone in this endeavor. Dale is the D in DP Stairlift, and his wife Pam is the P. Together they co-run the business, supporting and relying on one another along with their son Chris who works on the field with Dale as a technician. We are the definition of a home-grown, family business, and we like to reflect that dedication in our business practices.
